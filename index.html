Introduction

This visualization is about the world's human development index. HDI is an index created by the UNDP to measure national development. This visualiztion will give you a brief introduction to HDI and its relevant factors(GNI and life expectancy) and discover their relationship.

World's HDI in 2017

Australia's HDI is the highest(0.94) as well as Canada's HDI is the second highest(0.93). And most of developing countries's HDI are from 0.5 to 0.8. The lowest HDI is in Africa which 0.35.Looking at the GNI graph below, we can see that countries with higher GNI values also have higher HDI. World's HDI and its factors(GNI and Life expectancy) from 2011 to 2015

This graph shows the relationship between HDI, GNI and Life expectancy. We can see that most of the countries with a large HDI have a GNI around 2000 billion and a life expectancy between 80 and 85. However, over time, the life expectancy of all countries increases even though the GNI values do not change much. It can be found that HDI values increase with life expectancy and gni.
